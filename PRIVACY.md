# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 9, 2026**

## Overview

CallClip Video ("the App") is developed by Maksim Efremov. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The App operates entirely on your device. We do not:
- Collect personal information
- Track your usage
- Send analytics
- Use third-party tracking services
- Store any data on external servers

## Data Storage

All data created by the App (recordings, settings, preferences) is stored locally on your Mac. You have full control over this data.

## Camera and Microphone Access

The App requests access to your camera and microphone solely for the purpose of recording videos. This data is:
- Processed locally on your device
- Never transmitted to external servers
- Stored only where you choose to save it

## Screen Recording

If you use screen capture features, this data is:
- Processed locally on your device
- Never transmitted to external servers
- Stored only where you choose to save it

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at:
