# CallClip Video - Support

## About

CallClip Video is a professional split-screen video interview recording app for macOS.

## Features

- Split-screen recording
- Teleprompter for questions/scripts
- Subtitles overlay
- Background replacement with Ken Burns effect
- Smart split timing detection
- ProRes export

## System Requirements

- macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later
- Camera and microphone access
- Screen recording permission (for screen capture features)

## FAQ

### How do I grant screen recording permission?

1. Open System Settings → Privacy & Security → Screen Recording
2. Enable CallClip Video
3. Restart the app

### How do I replace the background?

1. Click on the background image area for Person 1 or Person 2
2. Select an image file
3. Optionally enable "Panning" for Ken Burns effect

### What video format is exported?

CallClip exports ProRes 422 LT (.mov) for high quality editing compatibility.

## Contact

## Privacy

CallClip Video does not collect any personal data. All recordings are stored locally on your device.
